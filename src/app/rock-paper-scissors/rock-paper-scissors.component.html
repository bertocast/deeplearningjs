How do I play?
First, you have to train the neural network to recognize what are you going to play: rock, paper or scissors. Press one of the 3 training buttons and hold it while the AI takes images and learn to classify the different shapes. Once it has seen at least 10 frames, you will be ready to go on to the next button. Try to move your hand around, in and out while the AI is taking pictures for a better accuracy. When you are done training the algorithm, you're ready to play. Click the Start a game button and keep your hand in front of the camera. The AI will recognize the shape of your hand and will do a random play.
